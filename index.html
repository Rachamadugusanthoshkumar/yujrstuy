Our facilities

The comfortable hostel facilities make one's stay at Vysya Students truly enjoyable. The institution provides independent hostel facilities for boys. The room are airy and furnished with comfortable facilities. People incharge of the Hostel share their care & concern to make it a homely place to stay and study. Vysya has expanded its hostel facilities to accommodate students, which makes the students to do a serious study and to grow spiritually, intellectually, physically & psychologically.

Accommodation

In the hostel provides a choice of three room types: A single room accommodating five shared occupants with five available carts, A single room for two shared occupants with two available carts, and Deluxe rooms accommodating four shared occupants with four available carts. Every room is equipped Windows or ventilation systems are in place to ensure proper airflow and fresh air circulation, and furnished with a table designed for educational purposes, along with a dedicated bookshelf.

Food

While the selection may not be extensive, it caters to the dietary needs of guests, particularly those looking for budget-friendly and vegetarian meal options.
